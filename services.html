30+ Light Vehicles
02
Light Vehicle Rental

Reliable Personnel Transport for Shift Rotations

Crew welfare and operational continuity begin with reliable, safe personnel transport. MKB's light vehicle fleet of 30+ units ensures that your teams move between camp, rig site, and support facilities with maximum efficiency and zero safety compromise.

All vehicles are company-owned, maintained to high safety and comfort standards, operated by licensed drivers, and registered within the CCPM system — ensuring full vehicle utilization tracking and compliance documentation for every project.

Double Cabin 4×4

Off-road capable field transport for supervisors, safety officers, and field inspection teams operating in remote terrain

Minibuses

Mid-capacity personnel transport for shift rotation between camp and rig site, maintained for comfort and reliability

Elf Buses

Larger crew transport for full-shift rotations, supporting 4× 550 HP and 5× 450 HP rig crew movements

Mobilization & De-mobilization

Precision Logistics From Origin to Field

A drilling campaign is only as effective as its mobilization. MKB provides comprehensive mobilization and demobilization management — from initial route surveys and permit coordination to final equipment staging, site setup, and post-project decommissioning. Every phase is managed by experienced logistics coordinators with deep familiarity with Indonesia's regulatory and physical terrain.

04
Project Coordination & Site Reporting

Operational Transparency Through Digital Integration

Modern upstream oil and gas operations demand real-time visibility and accountability. MKB's project coordination capability is built around full CCPM (Contractor Crew and Performance Management) integration — ensuring that every asset, personnel movement, and operational event is digitally captured, reported, and available to project stakeholders.

Daily Progress Reports

Structured daily reporting on equipment utilization, personnel hours, and task completion status

Downtime Tracking

Real-time monitoring and root-cause analysis of equipment or personnel downtime events

Cost-Efficiency Analysis

Per-unit cost tracking against project budget with variance reporting and corrective action workflows

Compliance Monitoring

Real-time CCPM compliance status across all assets and personnel — ready for client and regulator review
