Our Approach

Direct Ownership. Total Operational Control.

MKB's fleet strategy is built on a single, deliberate principle: full direct ownership. Every crane, every bulldozer, every double cabin in our fleet is owned outright by PT Mitra Kawan Bersama — not leased, not sub-contracted, not borrowed. This gives us complete operational control over maintenance schedules, deployment timelines, certification currency, and CCPM compliance status.

When a client commits to MKB, they gain access to a fleet that is certified, road-legal, permit-ready, and operationally available — not dependent on third-party availability or subject to competing project priorities. This is the MKB standard.
