Who We Are

Strategic Partner for Indonesia's Energy Sector

PT Mitra Kawan Bersama (MKB) is a performance-driven logistics and workforce solutions company built for the rigorous demands of upstream oil and gas operations. Since our founding in 2021, we have deployed over IDR 2 trillion in operational infrastructure — directly owning, certifying, and operating every asset in our fleet.

With full CCPM integration, MIGAS-certified personnel, and an unwavering commitment to ISO-standard quality, safety, and environmental management, MKB delivers the operational continuity that major energy projects demand.
