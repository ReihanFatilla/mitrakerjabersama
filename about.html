Who We Are

A Strategic Partner for Indonesia's Energy Sector

PT Mitra Kawan Bersama (MKB) is a performance-driven logistics and workforce solutions company engineered to meet the complex operational demands of Indonesia's upstream oil and gas sector. We were established in 2021 with a clear mandate: to deliver integrated, end-to-end field support that empowers energy operators to focus on extraction — while we handle the rest.

Unlike conventional contractors, MKB operates on a foundation of direct asset ownership. Every unit in our fleet is company-owned, independently certified, and registered within the CCPM digital management platform — giving our clients real-time operational visibility and full logistical accountability.

Backed by strong financial resources and an IDR 2 trillion+ operational infrastructure investment, MKB has built the scale and agility to serve both major national oil companies and regional energy operators across Indonesia — from the oil fields of Sumatra to projects across Central Java and beyond.

Investment in Growth

IDR 2 Trillion+ in Operational Infrastructure

Since inception, MKB has committed over IDR 2 trillion in operational infrastructure — a direct, strategic investment in the capability to deliver at the scale that Indonesia's most demanding energy projects require. This is not leased capacity. Every asset is owned, maintained, and deployed entirely under MKB's operational control.

Direct asset ownership is a deliberate strategic choice. It gives our clients cost certainty, mobilization speed, and operational reliability that third-party rental models cannot match. When a project demands a crawler crane at a remote Sumatran oil field at 48 hours' notice, MKB can deliver.
